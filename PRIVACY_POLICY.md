# Privacy Policy for Tree Tracker

**Last updated: March 7, 2026**

## Overview

Tree Tracker ("the App") is designed to help you record and track the locations of trees. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that data.

## Data Collection

### Data You Provide
- **Tree records**: Species, variety, rootstock, and notes you enter about trees
- **Photos**: Images you capture or select from your photo library to attach to tree records
- **Collections**: Names and organization of tree groupings you create

### Data Collected Automatically
- **GPS coordinates**: Precise location data captured when you record a tree, including latitude, longitude, altitude, and horizontal accuracy
- **Timestamps**: When tree records, notes, and photos are created or modified

### Data Not Collected
- The App does **not** collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics
- The App does **not** display advertising or share data with advertisers
- The App does **not** collect your name, email address, or any account information
- The App does **not** transmit your data to any third-party servers

## How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to provide the App's functionality:
- Displaying your tree records on maps and in lists
- Exporting your data in CSV, JSON, or GPX formats when you choose to share it
- Syncing your data across your own devices via iCloud (if enabled)

## Data Storage

### On-Device Storage
All tree records, photos, and notes are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

### iCloud Sync
If you are signed into iCloud, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. You can disable iCloud sync in your device's Settings.

### Apple Watch
If you use the companion watchOS app, tree data captured on your watch is transferred to your iPhone via Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This transfer happens directly between your devices.

## Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. The only ways data leaves the App are:
- **Export**: When you explicitly choose to export your data as CSV, JSON, or GPX files
- **iCloud Sync**: Between your own devices via your private iCloud account
- **Watch Sync**: Between your Apple Watch and iPhone

## Camera and Photo Library

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely to let you attach photos to tree records. Photos are stored within the App's data and are not uploaded to any external service.

## Location Data

The App requests location access solely to record GPS coordinates when you capture a tree. Location data is stored only within your tree records and is not transmitted to any external service. You can revoke location access at any time in your device's Settings.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device and in your iCloud account until you delete it. You can delete individual trees, notes, photos, or collections within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. To remove iCloud data, you can manage your iCloud storage in your device's Settings.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App does not require an account and collects no personal information beyond what the user explicitly enters.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
